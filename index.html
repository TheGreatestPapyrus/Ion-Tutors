An overview

Ion Tutors is a novel tutoring business, founded during the pandemic for the sole purpose of keeping children up to speed with education. Our goal is to bring academic excellence to your children, with a different approach from most tutoring businesses.

Our Method

At Ion Tutors, we take a slightly different approach towards educating your child; instead of merely just the classical one-on-one form of regular tutoring business, we offer a secondary service as well: Ion Workshops (click here for more information). At these workshops, a new and interesting concept in science, engineering, technology, or mathematics is explored, to spark curiosity in our students and inform them of something nouveau that they might be interested in.
