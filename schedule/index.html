Our Curriculum

What we teach is based directly off of the Ontario Elementary Curriculum. The subjects we will be teaching your children include the sciences science (and geography and history), technology, mathematics, and the the English language. However, we specialize in STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics) subjects, and will work to not only root a deep understanding of them within your child, but also to invoke in them a passion for these subjects.
