An overview

Ion Tutors is a novel tutoring business, founded during the pandemic for the sole purpose of keeping children up to speed with education. Our goal is to bring academic excellence to your children, with a different approach from most tutoring businesses.

Our method

The Ion Method

"The Ion Method" is our form of teaching, where we combine the benefits of regular schooling and tutoring into one. For the first two weeks, we will get to know your child better and do direct, one-on-one tutoring. After getting to know them, their skills, and what they might struggle with, we place them in our classroom environment to interact and learn with other children of similar abilities, hence not only promoting intellectual strength, but social strength as well.
